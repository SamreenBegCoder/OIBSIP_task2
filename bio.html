Biography

Ratan Tata, an Indian industrialist, philanthropist, and former chairman of Tata Sons, led the Tata Group, India's largest conglomerate, from 1991 to 2012 and again from October 2016 to February 2017. He is well known for his humility and visionary leadership, spearheading global expansions, such as the acquisition of Jaguar Land Rover and Tetley Tea.

Under his leadership, Tata Group’s revenues grew significantly, and the company became a global force in various industries including automobiles, steel, and information technology. Beyond business, he is known for his commitment to philanthropy, with a large portion of his wealth being channeled into education, rural development, and healthcare.
